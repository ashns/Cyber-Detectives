  • Social engineering: Fake urgency + impersonation made Emma hand over her password in under 60 seconds.

🔐 THE BIG TAKEAWAYS
  • Use a password manager — random passwords only, never personal info
  • Real companies never ask for your password over text or email
  • Check the sender's domain before trusting any urgent email
  • Check app permissions and reviews before installing anything free
  • Verify "friend" requests through a different channel before acting
